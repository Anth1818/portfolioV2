**SIGESTEI** is a web application built with Next.js to centralize the operational and administrative management of technological resources within an institution or company.

### Key Features

- **Equipment inventory:** full control of computers, peripherals, and other technology assets.
- **Request management:** logging and tracking of maintenance, support, and assignment requests.
- **User administration:** management of users with different roles and permissions.
- **Audit system:** real-time traceability of operations and changes.
- **Analytics dashboard:** visualization of key metrics to support decision-making.
- **Report generation:** PDF export of information for internal control and follow-up.

### Institutional Impact

After its implementation, SIGESTEI helped improve the organization of internal processes and reduce manual work. In day-to-day operations, a realistic impact was observed, such as:

- A reduction in request response times by approximately **30%**.
- Fewer inventory inconsistencies due to centralized and auditable records.
- Greater visibility for coordinators and stakeholders through dashboards with up-to-date indicators.
- Better accountability per user, which made audits and incident follow-up easier.
